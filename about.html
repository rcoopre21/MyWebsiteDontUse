Who I Am

My name is Reagan E. Cooper (Hence REC Art) and I'm an artist from Columbus, Ohio!
I'm 15 and I've been drawing for roughly 6 years. Currently, I am working to improve my art.
I am extremely passionate about art and am interested in studying history and/or art history
in college. My proudest acomplishment is when I was awarded an $100 scholarship for a pin
design for summer art classes at CCAD, the college in downtown Columbus.
