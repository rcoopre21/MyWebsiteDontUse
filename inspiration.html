My Inspiration

This page will display the inspirations I've taken from other artists.

Babs Tarr

Babs Tarr is a comic book artist who has worked on numerous things, such as the Batgirl comics.
She also draws for one of my current favorite comics, Motor Crush.

Christopher Burk

Christopher Burk is a visual artist from Columbus, Ohio who has had his work feautured in galleries all over Columbus.
He graduated from Columbus College of Art and Design. He is known for his paintings and drawings of buildings he takes pictures of.
